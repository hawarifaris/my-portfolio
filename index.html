About Me

Hello! I'm Faris Hawari Rahman, born on March 8, 2005, in Mataram. I have a profound passion for exploring new things and a deep love for playing the guitar. Currently, I reside in Desa Stuta, Kecamatan Janapria.

My educational journey began at SDN 2 Janapria, followed by my time as a student at a boarding school in Kapek Gunung Sari, West Lombok. I later graduated from SMAN 1 Janapria and am now pursuing my studies in Information Technology at a private university in Mataram.

My interest in technology grew as I observed the rapid advancements in the field, leading me to choose Information Technology as my major. My goal is to develop groundbreaking technologies similar to the blockchain technology behind cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin.

When I am not immersed in coding or studying, I spend my time discovering new melodies on my guitar or learning about the latest technological trends.
